QuickHist

Generate ASCII Histograms from the URL.

Type a list of comma-separated numbers to generate a histogram:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/25,50,75,100,75,50,25


Wanna insert labels? Separate the numbers and labels with an equals (=) sign:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/The Dude=100,Walter=133,Donny=138


Need a title? Type it out after the histogram data like so:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/The Dude=100,Walter=133,Donny=138/Bowling Scores


Try it: http://quickhist.onloop.net/

(Certain Special Characters like %, ? and # don't work. Negative numbers are made absolute.)


To get a plain, HTML-less histogram in your terminal, use curl and pipe the output to sed '1,2d;$d'

curl http://quickhist.onloop.net/The%20Dude=100,Walter=133,Donny=138 | sed '1,2d;$d' (Here's an example with filesystem sizes.)
