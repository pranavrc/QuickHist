QuickHist

Generate ASCII Histograms from the URL.


Type a list of comma-separated numbers to generate a histogram:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/25,50,75,100,75,50,25


Wanna insert labels? Separate the numbers and labels with an equals (=) sign:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/The Dude=100,Walter=133,Donny=138


Wanna insert a title? Type it out after the histogram data like so:

http://quickhist.onloop.net/The Dude=100,Walter=133,Donny=138/Bowling Scores
